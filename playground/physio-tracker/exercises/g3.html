Advanced ACL Prehab Guide

Follow these precise kinematic animations. The red highlights indicate the exact muscle group you should feel working. Move slowly and control the descent.

Phase 1: Activation & Mobility

Heel Slides Mobility

Slide your heel toward your body. Focus on closing the angle at the knee smoothly without lifting the foot off the ground.

Quad Sets VMO Focus

Squeeze the front of your thigh. The goal is to push the back of the knee absolutely flat against the mat.

Terminal Knee Ext. Quad/Stability

Using a resistance band, forcefully straighten the knee against the tension, squeezing the quad at the end.

Phase 2: Core Strength (The Muscle Splint)

Straight Leg Raise Hip/Quad

Lock the knee entirely straight first, then lift from the hip. Lower it slowly to resist gravity.

Glute Bridge Hamstrings/Glutes

Drive through the heels. Squeeze the glutes to lift the hips until your body forms a straight line.

Long Arc Quads Eccentric Control

From a seated position, fully extend the leg, squeeze, and slowly lower it back down.

Phase 3: Stability & Proprioception

Clamshells Glute Medius

Lie on your side. Keep ankles together and rotate the top knee upward to build hip stability.

Monster Walks Hip Abductors

Stay in a mini-squat. Step side-to-side, maintaining tension on the band around your ankles.

Bird-Dog Core/Anti-Rotation

From a tabletop position, slowly extend the opposite arm and leg perfectly straight. Do not arch the back.
