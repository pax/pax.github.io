Shared prehab goals

  • Calm swelling and pain.
  • Get the knee fully straight.
  • Regain flexion progressively.
  • Wake up the quadriceps and rebuild hip support.

If surgery happens

The same motion and strength work can help you arrive at surgery with a quieter knee and better early post-op readiness.

If surgery does not happen

The same base work still matters, then later drills usually shift toward balance, step control, squats, cycling, and gradual return to function.
